Betta Fish


Betta Fish is renowned for its mesmerizing appearance. With its flowing, feathery fins that resemble a half-moon when fully extended, this fish is a living work of art. Its scales shimmer with a myriad of colors, ranging from striking blues and deep reds to iridescent purples and opulent greens. Each Halfmoon Betta boasts a unique color pattern, making it a one-of-a-kind addition to your aquatic collection.
